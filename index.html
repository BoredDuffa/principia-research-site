About

Research infrastructure built for the questions your existing data cannot answer

Principia Research & Analytics is the analytical intelligence division of Principia Capital. We deliver the rigorous research output traditionally associated with major research institutions — applied to the specific requirements of corporate and public sector clients who require depth, precision, and genuine analytical independence.

Where large research houses deploy standardised methodologies at scale, we deploy concentrated expertise with proprietary frameworks designed entirely to the brief. Every engagement is original work.

"We do not produce templated reports. We build research infrastructure."

Our work spans machine learning research, bespoke algorithmic development, macroeconomic and sectoral analysis, quantitative financial modelling, and policy-facing research. We serve clients across the corporate sector — asset managers, financial institutions, operating businesses — and the public sector, including government-facing projects requiring legislative intelligence and economic modelling grounded in live data.

The team carries formal credentials from UCD, LSE, and HEC Paris, reinforced by an advisory board with decades across PwC, Invesco, PIMCO, and central bank-regulated environments.

Capabilities

Six research disciplines. No generalists.

Each engagement draws on the full depth of our quantitative and research infrastructure, constructed around the client's specific analytical requirement.

01
Machine Learning Research
We design and build original ML systems — Bayesian models, Riemannian manifold analysis, Kolmogorov complexity frameworks. Our work produces tools that operate in production environments, not academic proofs of concept. Every model is stress-tested, documented, and built for integration.
02
Proprietary Algorithm Development
We develop bespoke algorithmic frameworks for client-specific problems: scoring systems, predictive engines, classification models, and automated decision architecture. Algorithms are delivered with full documentation, benchmarking, and integration support.
03
Quantitative Financial Modelling
From advanced valuation including option pricing and econometric scenario analysis to portfolio construction and risk modelling — our quantitative output meets the standards of institutional asset management. We work to investment committee-grade precision.
04
Macro & Sectoral Research
Primary macroeconomic research covering Irish and international markets, sectoral deep-dives, and structural analysis. Independently sourced, rigorously evidenced, and delivered to a standard comparable with major research institutions.
05
Legislative & Policy Intelligence
Our proprietary macro engine monitors the Houses of the Oireachtas and European regulatory frameworks in real time — tracking policy shifts, identifying sectoral implications, and translating legislative change into precise, actionable intelligence.
06
Data Infrastructure & Scoring Systems
Scalable quantitative infrastructure: continuous data ingestion pipelines, proprietary scoring models operating at scale, and analytical systems designed for enterprise integration. AI-native architecture built to operate under production load.
